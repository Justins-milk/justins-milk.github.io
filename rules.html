Justin's Milk Frenzy: Rules

Here's how the game works:

  • You start with 3 HP. Every hit from an almond milk loses you 1 HP.
  • By pressing space, you can throw out your friend Ryan to absorb attacks for you. Hit an almond milk with Ryan three times to destroy it. You will shoot in the direction you are moving.
  • Parmesean cheese packets replenish your health; 1 HP each time you hit them, up to 3 HP per packet.
  • Be careful -- almond milks can also heal if they run into a cheese packet, and shooting one with Ryan will cause you to waste a potential heal.
  • Almond milks will spawn every 5 seconds, each time increasing their chase speed and their spawn number.
  • New cheese packets will spawn every 15 seconds for you to heal.
  • The game ends when you die and have your insides flooded with almond milk.
